Chapter 3: The Proposal

As the seasons changed in the mystical forest of Eldergrove, so did the love between Paulious and Robertina. The bond that had blossomed through dances and laughter, and endured the trials of separation, now faced a new chapter.
One evening, beneath the silver moonlight that peeked through the leaves, Paulious led Robertina to a secluded glade adorned with vibrant flowers. The air was filled with the sweet scent of blossoms, and a gentle breeze whispered through the trees, as if nature itself was conspiring to make this moment special.
"Robertina," Paulious began, his voice filled with a mixture of nervousness and excitement, "these past years have been a journey of growth, of love, and of finding each other in the most unexpected ways."
Robertina looked at him, her eyes reflecting the moonlight, curiosity and anticipation dancing in them.
"I can't imagine my life without you," Paulious continued, his hand reaching into a small pouch at his side. He pulled out a delicate silver ring, adorned with a moonstone that shimmered like the night sky.
"Robertina," he said, dropping to one knee, "will you make me the happiest elf in Eldergrove and marry me?"
A gasp escaped from Robertina's lips as she took in the sight before her. Her heart raced, and she felt a mix of emotions swirling within her, joy, love, and peace.
Paulious gazed up at her, his eyes reflecting both hope and vulnerability. "I want to spend the rest of my life with you, to dance through the seasons hand in hand, facing whatever challenges may come our way."
The moonlight seemed to pause, as if holding its breath for Robertina's answer. A gentle rustle in the leaves and the distant melody of a nightingale created a symphony of nature, adding to the magic of the moment.
The delicate ring sparkled in the moonlight, waiting to be accepted and to symbolize a union that could withstand the tests of time.
In that suspended moment, the forest held its breath, and the moon witnessed a proposal wondering what the response will be.
